Non Vegetarian Weight Loss Meal

Following a non-vegan diet plan for weight loss is right for your health as this type of diet provides your body with the required amounts of calcium, which helps in making your bones stronger.The non-vegetarian food items are a rich source of protein, and therefore, it supports lean muscle gain. Eggs and other non-veg food items are great for speeding up the muscle recovery process of your body

Meal Plan-

  • Meal 1 (8:00 AM): Jasmine Tea or Tulsi Decoction /Lemon Water( Luke warm without sugar/salt/honey)
  • Meal 2 (11:00 AM): Jasmine Tea or Tulsi Decoction /Lemon Water( Luke warm without sugar/salt/honey)
  • Meal 3 (2:00 PM): Orange or Seasonal Fruit+ Buttermilk / Roasted Chana Chat (1 fist)
  • Meal 4 (5:00 PM): Orange or Seasonal Fruit+ Buttermilk / Roasted Chana Chat (1 fist). Chapatti/Missi Roti/Any Stuffed Roti without oil
  • Meal 5 (8:00 PM): Chicken Soup (homemade) /Green tea + Marie Biscuit -2
  • Meal 6 (11:00 PM): Boiled chicken / Roasted Chicken Mixed salad

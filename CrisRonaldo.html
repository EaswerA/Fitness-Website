Cristiano Ronaldo





Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the greatest football players of his generation. The winner of five Ballon d’Or awards, he is among the sport’s top goal scorers. Ronaldo was one of the most well-known sports stars off the field, and numerous studies of athletes’ popularity showed that he was the most-beloved athlete in the world during his playing peak

Ronaldo is a formidable athlete on the pitch. Originally a right-winger, he developed into a forward with a free-reined attacking style. He was able to mesmerize opponents with a sleight of foot that made sufficient space for openings in opposing defenses. He has won numerous Club level trophies such as the UEFA Champions League and even International trophies such as the Euros.





Training Regime

Besides long and intense team practices and footwork training, Cristiano focuses on improving his strength, stamina, speed, and functional movements.“Learn to train your mind as well as your body,” he once said. “Mental strength is just as important as physical strength and will help you achieve your goals.” Pair all that with eight hours of sleep a night and you’re living strong in every sense of the concept.

Diet and Nutrition

Cristiano Ronaldo maintains a wonderfully diverse diet, he gets down with fruit juices, bread, fish, eggs, steak, and the occasional slice of cake or chocolate bar. Despite his seemingly bottomless appetite, Cristiano Ronaldo nevertheless takes a hard line when it comes to diet and nutrition, especially whilst training. For example, he typically divides his daily food intake into six smaller meals, which he consumes at intervals of 2-4 hours. This not only sustains a consistent and optimal rate of metabolism, but it prevents the body from experiencing weakness and hunger throughout the day.

Non Vegetarian Weight Gain Meal

Non-Vegetarian foods are generally more calorie dense than plant-sourced foods. Hence, it is easier to gain weight through a non-vegetarian diet and build muscle

Meal Plan-

  • Meal 1 (8:00 AM): Oats (2/3 cup) Granola or cereals (1/4 cup) Whey protein (2/3 spoon) Omelet (1 whole + 1 white) with Whole wheat bread (1 slice)
  • Meal 2 (11:00 AM): Cranberries (1/8 cup) Almonds – crushed (1/8 cup) Walnuts – crushed (1/8 cup) Banana (1/2)
  • Eggs while – scrambled (3) Fresh paneer (50 g) Sweet potatoes (149 g) Kidney beans (1/2 cup) Spinach – chopped (1/4 cup) Green onion – chopped (3 spoons)
  • Meal 4 (5:00 PM): Yogurt (2 spoons) Whey protein (2/3 spoon) Honey (1 spoon)
  • Meal 5 (8:00 PM): Chicken Drumsticks (2 legs) Kidney Beans (1/4 cup) Chickpeas (1/4 cup) Chopped carrots (2 spoons) Chopped tomato (2 spoons)
  • Meal 6 (11:00 PM): Egg white (1 large) Casein protein or whey protein (2/3 scoop) Raisins (1 spoon).

Healthy Diet Plans

Vegetarian Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits, chia seeds, and almond milk.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and almond butter.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with mixed vegetables, chickpeas, and a lemon-tahini dressing.
  • Afternoon Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
  • Dinner: Lentil soup with whole grain bread and a side of roasted vegetables.
  • Evening Snack: A handful of mixed nuts and an apple.

Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado toast.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and blueberries.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and a balsamic vinaigrette.
  • Afternoon Snack: Sliced bell peppers with guacamole.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli.
  • Evening Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks.
